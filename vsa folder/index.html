Welcome to WAYUPARTY , the ultimate clubbing companion that puts the world of nightlife at your fingertips!

Are you ready to embark on an electrifying journey through the pulsating beats and vibrant scenes of the nightlife? Look no further! WAYUPARTY
is here to revolutionize the way you experience clubbing, making sure every night out becomes an unforgettable adventure.

Cheers

"Cheers" is a feature that allows the user to book alcohol. It can be used to book a bottle or a pint.

Save A Seat

"Save A Seat" is a feature that can be used to book a table. The user will have the option to select the alcohol and food for his table while booking it.

Make A Jump

"Make A Jump" allows that user to book entries to the club. The user will be given a QR code to scan at the club to grant them entry.

Make it Personal

"Make it Personal" makes it much easier to plan a surprise party for your loved ones! The user can select from an array of options offered by the venue to plan the most memorable surprise.

Deals

“Deals “ helps the user easily access offers in a venue such as happy hours or ladies nights! And Venue can reach out to the customers in real time about the deals and offer.

The Wayuparty

" The Wayuparty” is for corporate events or gatherings of huge numbers. We offer package deals under this feature for such events.

Events

"Events" is a feature to showcase different events in the city. The user can book passes or tickets for the event on the app.

Food

“Food” Don’t let hunger ruin your party, Pre book your food.

Be A Vip

“Be a VIP” Ready to Stand out and explore new heights of party with Luxury. It allows the user to book the services at clubs as well as limousine which will pick up and drop the customer at the given address.
